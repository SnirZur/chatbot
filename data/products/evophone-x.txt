EvoPhone X is a fictional flagship smartphone built around a 6.6-inch OLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and 2400x1080 resolution. The device uses an AeroCore A9 chipset with 8 GB or 12 GB of RAM and storage options of 128 GB or 256 GB. The chassis is aluminum with GorillaGlass-like protection and an IP68 water resistance rating.

Battery capacity is 4,800 mAh. Typical mixed-use battery life is listed as 24 hours, with video playback up to 18 hours. The device supports 45W wired fast charging and 15W wireless charging. A full wired charge is advertised at around 55 minutes with a compatible charger.

The camera system includes a 50 MP main sensor with optical image stabilization, a 12 MP ultra-wide lens, and an 8 MP telephoto lens with 3x optical zoom. The front camera is 16 MP. The phone supports 4K video at 60 fps and a low-light mode called NightPulse.

Connectivity includes 5G sub-6, Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.3, and NFC. Dual-SIM is supported (one physical SIM plus one eSIM). The EvoPhone X runs OrionOS 5.1 with a promised three years of major updates.
